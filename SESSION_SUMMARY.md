# Pocket Foreman Growth Sprint: March 2026

### **Phase 1: Paid Ads (Day 1-2)**

*   **Objective:** Diagnose and fix the 0% conversion rate on a high-CTR Reddit ad campaign.
*   **Actions Taken:**
    *   Identified a key friction point: a two-step signup process.
    *   Implemented an inline signup form, added social proof, and improved the page title for better "scent" from ad to landing page.
    *   Upgraded the agent's underlying model to `openrouter/google/gemini-2.5-pro-preview` for better cost-effectiveness.
*   **Outcome:** While optimizations were successful, a forecast showed that achieving the desired user numbers through paid ads at this stage would be capital-intensive.

---

### **Phase 2: The Pivot - Organic Growth (Day 3-10)**

*   **New Objective:** Validate Product-Market Fit by acquiring **10+ users in the next 7 days** through low-cost, organic methods.
*   **Hypothesis:** The core value proposition is strong enough to attract early adopters without significant ad spend. If not, the product may not be viable.
*   **Strategy: The "10 Users in 7 Days" Organic Growth Sprint**
    1.  **Weaponize Messaging:** Draft core content around the "Stop managing projects over text" theme.
    2.  **Community Engagement:** Identify and engage in relevant online communities (subreddits, Facebook groups, forums) *before* pitching.
    3.  **Direct Outreach:** Craft a "founder's offer" seeking honest feedback, not a sale, to be sent to a targeted list of small construction companies.
    4.  **Content Launch:** Share the blog post in the warmed-up communities.
    5.  **Build in Public:** Share the story and progress publicly to attract supporters.

---

### **Current Status (as of 2026-03-13)**

*   **Status:** The pivot to organic growth has been initiated. The paid ad campaign is conceptually paused.
*   **Next Step:** Begin Phase 2, Step 1 & 2: Draft the core blog post and identify key online communities for contractor engagement.
